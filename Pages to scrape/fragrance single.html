8th Wonder by French Avenue is a Oriental Woody fragrance for women and men. This is a new fragrance. 8th Wonder was launched in 2025. Top notes are Pink Pepper, Cardamom and Candy Apple; middle notes are Liquor, Boozy Notes, Dates, Davana and Osmanthus; base notes are Amber Xtreme, Myrrh, Labdanum, Benzoin, Styrax and Patchouli.
